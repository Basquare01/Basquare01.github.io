About Binnaf

BINNAF stands as a beacon of excellence in the field of workplace safety and health education. Renowned globally for its unwavering commitment to fostering a culture of safety,BINNAF has garnered international recognition for its comprehensive training programs, cutting-edge research, and dedication to professional development. Registered with CAC in May,23rd,2016 and Corporate Member with OSHAssociation UK, Nigeria Region, with International Membership No.243940 in April,30th, 2024,as (Authorised Training Provider/IAT). With a VISION; to "Mitigate Workplace Hazards" and MISSION; to "Promote a Safer Working Environment" BINNAF; has emerged as a leader in occupational safety and health education. Equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and staffed by industry experts and seasoned practitioners, the centre offers a diverse array of training courses tailored to meet the evolving needs of various sectors and industries. What sets BINNAF apart is, its emphasis on practical,hands-on learning experiences coupled with theoretical knowledge. Participants not only gain a deep understanding of safety principles and regulations but also acquire the skills necessary to implement them effectively in real-world scenarios. This holistic approach ensures that Graduates/Participants are equipped with the confidence and competence to address safety challenges proactively.
